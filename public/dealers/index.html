The Founding Charter

Three commitments. Written into every dealer agreement.

Not a marketing promise. Binding terms at signup — part of the operating agreement every dealer receives.

Commitment 01

Lead Ownership

Every buyer enquiry is delivered directly to you. Selectus does not intercept, qualify, re-route, or monetise your leads. This is not a policy that can be changed by a product update.

Commitment 02

Search Integrity

Organic search results are ordered by relevance and recency only. No dealer can pay to appear above a more relevant listing. Any sponsored placements are visually distinct and clearly labelled.

Your listing history, enquiry records, and contact data belong to you. Request a full export at any time. Leaving Selectus Motors costs nothing and loses nothing.
